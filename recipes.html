Feijoada

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams (1 pound) of dried black beans
  • 500 grams (1 pound) of pork ribs
  • 500 grams (1 pound) of carne seca (dried beef)
  • 250 grams (1/2 pound) of smoked sausage (linguiça)
  • 250 grams (1/2 pound) of bacon
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (enough to cover the beans and meats)
  • Farofa (toasted manioc flour) for serving
  • Rice for serving
  • Orange slices for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the black beans and soak them overnight in water. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, add the soaked beans and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil and cook for about 1 hour or until the beans are tender. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a separate pot, add the pork ribs and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. In another pot, add the carne seca and enough water to cover it. Bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a large skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add the smoked sausage and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  6. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  7. Add the cooked pork ribs, carne seca, smoked sausage, and bay leaves to the skillet with the sautéed onions and garlic. Cook for a few minutes to combine the flavors.
  8. Transfer the meat mixture to the pot with the cooked black beans. Add enough water to cover the beans and meats. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until the meats are tender and the flavors are well blended. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Remove the bay leaves before serving.
  10. Serve the feijoada hot with steamed rice, farofa, and orange slices on the side.
